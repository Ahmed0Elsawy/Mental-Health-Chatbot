Our Process

Step 1: Datasets

Mental Health in Social Media: Data from tweets, forums, and mental health-related posts. Reddit data via Pushshift API for subreddits like r/mentalhealth or r/depression. Cornell Movie Dialogues Corpus: For conversational training.

Step 2: Tools and Libraries

Hugging Face Transformers: Pretrained conversational AI models like GPT or Llama. FastAPI or Flask: Lightweight backend frameworks for chatbot logic. Deployment: Use Streamlit Cloud or Heroku for free, scalable hosting.

Step 3: Learning Resources

Hugging Face Tutorials: Step-by-step AI chatbot development. Mental health guidelines: Mind.org.uk and WHO Mental Health Resources. Courses: Coursera NLP Specialization.
